Hi. I’m Reyn Alpha. Welcome to my website.

Who am I? Great question. I am an Icelandic teenager and I spend most of my time on the computer doing various things. I am not going to list all of them but in particular I like video editing, programming and web development. You can check out some of what I’ve made on my YouTube channel and on my Web Experiments blog. I am currently an undergraduate student in computer science at the University of Iceland.

Now, you may be asking yourself: “What is the purpose of this website?”
To answer that question, I’m not sure. I just thought it would be a good idea to have a website since I’ve had an online presence for the past decade. Also, it’s better in my opinion to have a landing page for my domain rather than having Apache spit out an empty directory listing or creating a lazy index.html file saying something clever about there being nothing to see here.

If you want to contact me for any reason, you can do so on this website. You can also check out my social media pages if you want. And if you’re bored, you can play a game by performing the Konami code. Try it!
